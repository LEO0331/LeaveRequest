Privacy & Security Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Data Storage

This application stores leave-request records in your browser local storage using leave-requests-v1. Data does not automatically leave your browser unless you export files.

Data Export & Import

  • CSV and PDF exports are generated locally in your browser.
  • CSV imports are validated for format and size, and invalid rows are reported in an error file.
  • Exported files may contain personal information depending on your records.

Security Controls

  • Input validation for date ranges, required fields, overlap checks, and leave balance checks.
  • CSV export sanitization to reduce spreadsheet formula injection risks.
  • Role-based workflow actions for employee and manager flows.

Retention & Deletion

You can remove records by using delete actions in the UI or by clearing browser storage for this site.

Contact

For questions about privacy or security handling, contact your system administrator or project owner.
